About

From CRUST to ProntoBites.

With ProntoBites, Klime and Anita Kovaceski bring forward the experience, standards, and reputation they built through CRUST Restaurant in Miami — a concept known for both its dining experience and its successful delivery and takeout operations. This new concept reflects everything we've learned over the years while offering something more streamlined and present-day: a fresh approach to delivery, takeout, and quick bites, with a casual space to stop in and enjoy a quick meal — all backed by the same commitment to quality that has always guided us.

Quick-Bite Dining

A casual space to stop in.

ProntoBites is primarily an Italian delivery and carry-out concept, while also offering a casual quick-bite dining space for lunch and dinner. Pre-order online 24/7 through our website or place an order at the counter for pickup. No reservations, no table service — simply quality food made fresh and served efficiently. A self-service station with utensils, napkins, and condiments will be available on-site. Walk-ins are always welcome, but pre-ordering is strongly encouraged for the fastest experience.

Investors & Licensing

An early-stage concept built to scale.

Email about licensing (786) 566-0498

ProntoBites is an early-stage, delivery and takeout-focused food concept in Miami, built on proven restaurant experience and a scalable, modern operating model. We are currently open to conversations with potential investors and partners interested in supporting growth, expansion, and licensing opportunities.

Beyond Miami, the concept is designed with multi-market scalability in mind — suitable for expansion into additional cities and states across the U.S. The operational model, brand structure, and streamlined kitchen workflow allow ProntoBites to be replicated efficiently in diverse urban and suburban markets, adapting to local demand while maintaining consistent quality and service.

We also continue to own the CRUST brand (CrustAmerica.com) and CRUST2Go (Crust2go.com), which extend our intellectual property and brand portfolio beyond the ProntoBites concept. These remain active brand assets supporting future licensing, partnership, and multi-market expansion opportunities.
